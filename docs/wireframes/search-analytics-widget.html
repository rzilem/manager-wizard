Design Specifications

  • KPI Cards: 4 primary metrics with trend indicators
  • Chart: Recharts AreaChart for 7-day volume trend
  • Failed Queries: Priority indicators (red=critical, orange=high, yellow=medium)
  • Top Searches: Horizontal bars showing success rate
  • Recommendations: Action-oriented cards with Create Doc / Dismiss buttons
  • Time Filter: Today, 7 Days, 30 Days toggle
  • Color Palette: Indigo primary (#6366f1), success green, warning amber, danger red
